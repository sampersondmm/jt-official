FULL BODY MASSAGE BENEFITS

We all know that getting a massage feels good. However, most people dont know that getting a massage has many physical and psychological health benefits as well. Some of the most recognized benefits include reduction in muscle tension, relaxation, and stress relief. 75-90% of all doctor visits are stress related.

There are many more excellent reasons to choose massage therapy for your health. Several conditions can be prevented, treated or resolved through massage and self care instead of through surgery or pharmaceuticals, which can be ineffective, risky, or come with undesirable side effects. J Therapy seeks to target and treat the source(s) of specific problems, whereas many pain medicines can only mask the sensation of pain, and surgery can in many cases be completely avoided.

Massage therapy can:
-relieve anxiety
-decrease depression
-ease and decrease the frequency of tension headaches and migraines
-increase range of motion
-improve cardiovascular health
-improve respiratory health
-lower blood pressure
-decrease rheumatoid arthritis pain
-ease nausea and digestive disorders
-help relieve soft tissue strains and injuries
-help treat carpel tunnel and tarsal tunnel
-promote calmness and mental clarity
-decrease insomnia and sleep disorders

Massage can also assist with:
-TMJ
-fibromyalgia
-muscular, fascial and scar tissue adhesions
-myofascial pain syndrome
